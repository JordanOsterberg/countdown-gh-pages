Readily Available.

Countdown is now available on the Mac App Store. That makes Countdown available on three platforms‐ iOS, macOS, watchOS.


Light, and Dark.

Countdown looks great with both Light and Dark mode in macOS Mojave, complimenting the look of your Mac.


Designed for Mac.

Countdown for Mac is exactly that— for Mac. It’s a full-featured app, built from the ground up specifically for macOS.


In Sync.

Countdown now syncs between all of your devices— iPad, iPhone, and Mac using iCloud. Free of charge, and private.


Re-written.

We’ve re-written a majority of the code to keep your experience as smooth as possible.
